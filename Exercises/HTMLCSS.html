Units

What different units can you use to specify text size in CSS? What do they mean? What does anything mean, when you get right down to it?

  1. cm (centimeters)
  2. mm (millimeters)
  3. in (inches) [1in = 96px = 2.54cm]
  4. px (pixels) [pixels (1px = 1/96th of 1in)]
  5. pt (points) [points (1pt = 1/72 of 1in)]
  6. pc picas [picas (1pc = 12 pt)]
